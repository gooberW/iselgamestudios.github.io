We are

ISEL Game Studios

ISEL Game Studios is a club dedicated to game development, founded in 2025 by second-year students of the Informatics and Multimedia Engineering BSc (LEIM) at ISEL. We believe that creating games goes beyond programming, which is why we bring together students from different areas, such as art and sound, to collaborate and learn together.

Our goal is to provide an environment where members can learn, experiment, and collaborate, regardless of their level of experience. Whether you're a programmer, artist, designer, or just curious about the world of video games, any student from the Polytechnic Institute of Lisbon (IPL) can join us and participate in our projects and events.

Throughout the year, we organize game jams, workshops, lectures, and collaborative projects, helping members develop their skills and gain practical experience in the video game industry.

If you're interested in developing games and working in a team, join us and start creating!
