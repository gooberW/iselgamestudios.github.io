Nature Strikes Back

“Nature Strikes Back” is one of the projects currently in development at the club, with its debut scheduled for Lisboa Games Week 2025. It is a top-down shooter inspired by the aesthetic of 1950s B-movies, combining intense action with a touch of retro humor. In this game, the protagonist, “MAN,” faces a horde of plant monsters that have invaded his city, in an epic battle between humanity and the fury of nature.

After a while in development, we finally launched the Nature Strikes Back demo! Try it out now at Nature Strikes Back
